Tribute to Salman Khan

About Salman Khan

Salman Khan is an Indian film actor, producer, and television personality. He is one of the biggest stars in Bollywood and has been entertaining audiences for decades. Known for his charismatic screen presence and philanthropy, Salman has a massive fan following.

Early Life

Salman Khan was born on December 27, 1965, in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, India. He comes from a family with a strong film industry background, and his father, Salim Khan, is a renowned screenwriter.

Film Career

Salman Khan's career began with a supporting role in the 1988 film "Biwi Ho To Aisi." He rose to prominence with the 1989 film "Baaghi" and solidified his stardom with the 1990s blockbuster "Hum Aapke Hain Koun..!" Salman has been a part of numerous hit films like "Bajrangi Bhaijaan," "Kick," "Sultan," and many others.

Salman Khan's Image

Salman Khan's style and image have always been a topic of discussion, both on and off-screen.

His fitness and well-maintained physique have made him an icon in the industry.
